Open Psychology is devoted to improving reproducibility in the social, and other, sciences. Accordingly, this site was designed with the following specific goals.

  • Create an reasonably open source platform for conducting experimental, descriptive and correlational research online.
  • Provide open access to a variety of classic psychological experiments, measures, and the resultant data-sets for students, instructors, and/or researchers to use as they see fit.
  • Develop collaborative relationships with primary investigators who wish to develop new online instruments.

If you're a student, who is interested in participating in an experiment for course-credit, or a random visitor, who is interested in participating just for fun, please check the list of current experiments. We only have one now, but, hopefully, we'll have more soon.

If you're a researcher who would like to collaborate or wants more information about this platform, please contact Open Psychology.
